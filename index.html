Introduction

Hey there! I’m An, but you can also call me Hako or Cún. I’m a student currently grinding at FPS TP.HCM (K20). I’m low-key obsessed with tech and love building and designing things on the web. Alongside my practical experience as a Web Developer working with HTML/CSS and C programming, I’m deeply into UI/UX design. I always pay close attention to mobile responsive standards, color theory, and typography, usually aiming for a soft, pastel anime aesthetic in my projects. Feel free to check out my repository on GitHub.

When I’m not coding or revising my English, I’m usually chilling on Roblox or queuing for Valorant—honestly, I spent way too much time in Aim Lab just to fix my aim and train my reaction time. I’m also a massive Honkai: Star Rail fan, and my absolute fave character is Evanescia. The insane graphics and character design in that game actually give me so much inspo for my own web interfaces.
